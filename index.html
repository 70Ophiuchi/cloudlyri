Glossary

What is a free consultation?

Don’t see what you need? Our Theta and Vega subscribers get free consultation(s) with a professional Cloud Engineer every month. The Cloud Engineer will share a diagram of the infrastructure best suited to your requirements. Spots for the free consultation are limited each month. Response time can vary depending on traffic.

How does the payment model work?

Our platform fees are fixed, there are 3 subscription models as stated above Kappa, Theta, and Vega. AWS Services fees are not included in the cost. An estimate will be provided to you before you deploy your architecture, and a hold will be placed on your card, only the actual amount will be charged at the end of each month.

What is an AWS Service?

An AWS Service is an offering by Amazon Web Services for a certain amount of money. An example of a service would be an EC2 server or an S3 bucket. Please navigate to our explaining AWS cloud concepts page to learn more about the different types of services.

What are AWS Services fees?

AWS charges a fixed amount of money when you use their services, this amount varies depending on the type of service and it will be explicitly declared BEFORE you deploy your desired infrastructure.

Solutions

Deploying your website to the cloud has never been easier with our platform. With just a few clicks, you can have your website up and running in the cloud, accessible to users all over the world.

Here are the steps on how to deploy your website to the cloud through our platform:
  1. Select a plan and create your account.
  2. Upload your website files to your account.
  3. Specify the framework that your website is built on.
  4. Click the "Deploy" button.
  5. That's it! Your website will be up and running in the cloud in just a few minutes.
Static and WordPress websites are also supported.

Need a server? Lyri makes it easy to rent and launch a server in the cloud, forget having to go through numerous steps just to launch a server, we've made the process simple and easy on our platform.

Here are the steps on to deploy a server in the cloud using our platform:
  1. Select a plan and create your account.
  2. Select the type of server you need.ThetaVega
  3. Choose your operating system
  4. Choose whether you want the server to have a Public IP* or not.
  5. Click the "Launch Server" button
  6. Your server will be available in a few minutes. (Note: The credentials to SSH into your server will be emailed to you)
A fully configured VPN server deployment option is available in the Theta plan onwards.

*If you want your server to have internet access please tick the Public IP option

Using cloud storage through our platform is easy and convenient. With our platform, you can store your files in the cloud and access them from anywhere.

Here are the steps on how to use cloud storage through our platform:
  1. Select a plan and create your account.
  2. Upload your files to your account using the "Upload" button.
  3. Once your files are uploaded, you can request a shareable link by clicking the "Share" button
  4. Delete or download files using the "Delete" and "Download" buttons

Get a free consultation every month with a professional cloud engineer through our platform. Our team of experts is available to answer your questions and help you troubleshoot any issues you may have.

Here are the steps to redeem your monthly free consultation:
  1. Select a plan and create your account.
  2. Click the "Consult with an Engineer" button.
  3. Select the date and time of your consultation.
  4. Describe your issue in the provided form.
  5. Submit your request.
Only available in our Theta (1x free consultation) and Vega (2x free consultations) plans.

Additional paid consultations are available on request

Creating a CI/CD pipeline in the cloud is easy with our platform. Our platform makes it simple to automate your software development process, so you can focus on building great software.

Here are the steps on how to create a CI/CD pipeline in the cloud through our platform:
  1. Select a plan and create your account.
  2. Connect your code repository to our platform or create a code repository by clicking the "Create Repository" button.
  3. Specify build commands. (You can refer to our detailed guide for more information)
  4. Enter your Email Address for build notifications
  5. Click the "Create Pipeline" button
  6. You will be notified via email when your pipeline is available.
